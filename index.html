Introduction

Bunny Blasters is an Overwatch team ran by a slightly unstable coach. The name is an inside joke but the player behind the inside joke does not approve of the name.

Requirements

  • Rating

    Your rating must be below 3600 to join. If you are 3100 or below, you may still try out, but you are strongly encouraged to have scrim experience to have the best chance of passing the tryout.

  • Availability

    You must be available at least 3 days of the week, and preferably 4 days. Occasional absences are fine, but just don't make it a pattern.

  • Responsibilities

    All players are responsible for showing up at not only scrims, but tournament matches and team VOD reviews. You must also set up a time for individual VOD reviews if asked.
